Plan and structure multi-chapter educational books with optimal cognitive load management and learning progression.

WHAT IT DOES:
Creates complete book structures with chapters, sections, and subsections organized for progressive learning. Manages complexity distribution and ensures prerequisites flow logically.

STRUCTURE ELEMENTS:
- Part/Section divisions (group related chapters)
- Chapter organization (week-by-week or topic-based)
- Learning arc (introduction → mastery)
- Dependency mapping (what builds on what)
- Cognitive load distribution (easy → hard → easy)
- Checkpoint placement (assessments, projects)

BOOK ARCHITECTURE:
Part I: Foundations (weeks 1-2)
Part II: Core Skills (weeks 3-7)
Part III: Advanced Topics (weeks 8-12)
Part IV: Integration & Capstone (week 13)

USE FOR: Designing overall textbook structure, planning chapter sequences, organizing curriculum.
